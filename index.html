Welcome to Learning Tree House Kindergarten

A joyful place where young minds blossom through play, learning, and creativity!

About Us

Learning Tree House Kindergarten nurtures children aged 3-6 in a warm, tree-inspired environment. Our dedicated teachers encourage curiosity, teamwork, and a love for learning through hands-on, play-based activities.

Come grow with us in our tree house of knowledge!

Our Programs

Preschool

Engaging activities for 3-4 year olds, including crafts, stories, and early math.

Pre-K

Building skills for 4-5 year olds with reading, writing, and social development.

After-School Care

Fun and creative support for kids up to age 6, with activities and homework help.
